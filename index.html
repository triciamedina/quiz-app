Blockchain technology is neither a company nor an app. At its most basic level, a blockchain is a growing list of records containing digital information (the “blocks”), which are linked using cryptography. Data contained within the blockchain is stored in a public database distributed across computers in a network so that any involved record cannot be altered retroactively, without the alteration of all subsequent blocks.
